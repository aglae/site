Collected observations on systems that don't announce themselves:

A good thermostat is invisible until the moment you're uncomfortable. A good design system is invisible until the moment someone ships something off-brand. Quiet systems earn their silence by working; loud systems demand attention to compensate for not working.

The corollary: if a system needs a dashboard to prove it's alive, ask who the dashboard is really for.
